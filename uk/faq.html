Bravospeed is a free lottery game. Is this legal?

Only lotteries that you pay to play are under state monopoly in France. Since Bravospeed is free, it does not fall under this monopoly and is therefore perfectly legal (Article L322-2 of the French Internal Security Act, or Code de la sécurité intérieure).

Ok, so it’s legal... But is it trustworthy?

These four factors should give you every confidence in Bravospeed:

- The bailiff. Every draw is supervised by a bailiff. They have access to the draw program and can activate it manually.

- The certification. The randomness of the draw has been certified by an independent evaluation company. We chose one of the companies with the most stringent standards, authorised by the French Online Gambling Regulator (ARJEL).

- The insurer. The big Jackpot prizes are covered by an insurance company. Whenever a player wins the Gold or Diamond Jackpot, it’s our insurer who pays out. It’s in our best interest if you win the jackpot!

How are the prizes funded?

Like most apps these days, the funding comes from advertising.
Ads pay for the prizes and the insurance on all big Jackpot prizes!

Can I actually win money?

We take the utmost care to ensure that winners receive their winnings as quickly as possible – our reputation depends on it!
For cash prizes, our team will send you a message within 48 hours (and often much sooner)! We will ask for your bank details and make a payment directly into your bank account.
Bravospeed has only been live for a short time, so it currently boasts fewer winners than our other app which has thousands, Bravoloto – but that won’t last long!

Do I really have a chance of winning?

Just like any other lottery game, luck is the most important factor in Bravospeed!
To take away the Jackpot (Bronze, Silver, Gold or Diamond), you certainly need luck on your side! The odds of winning the big Jackpots in Bravospeed are the same as in other lotteries that you pay to play, but seeing as you can play hundreds of free grids every single day, your chances of winning are actually multiplied!

How do I play my grids?

There are two ways to play a grid in Bravospeed: either by submitting it directly for the next draw or by saving it for later.
A button allows you to choose at the bottom of the “Play a grid” page.

If you decide to save your grid, you can validate it for the next draw at any time, either via the button in the bottom right of the home page, or via the congratulations page displayed after completing a grid.
Saving grids allows you to accumulate multiple grids for a specific draw and increase your chances of winning it. You can save up to 100 grids.

How do I win the jackpot?

During the draw, 7 Speed Balls will be drawn at random, followed by 3 Bonus Balls.
Your need 4 correct Speed Balls + the 3 correct Bonus Balls to win the Jackpot.

How many draws are there? And when do they happen?

There are 4 types of Jackpot in Bravospeed:

- Bronze Jackpot: one draw every 3 minutes and €5,000 to be won at Tier 1
- Silver Jackpot: one draw every 10 minutes and €50,000 to be won at Tier 1
- Gold Jackpot: one draw every hour and €500,000 to be won at Tier 1
- Diamond Jackpot: one draw every hour and €5 million to be won at Tier 1.

How do I get tickets?

We will give you “Bronze” tickets automatically: a countdown timer on the “Bronze” page indicates when you will receive your next tickets.
There are several ways to get Silver, Gold and Diamond tickets!
- Get them in the Prize Store with “Speed Points” earned from winning draws!
- Inviting your friends
- Installing partner applications
